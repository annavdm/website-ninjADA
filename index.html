Anatomy of a Hater

Now that the dataset has no more secrets lets dive into the hater hunting process. The idea here is to start from the embeddings dataset and cluster them into communities to see if there are hater-friendly themes coming out.

\Geopolitics

Haters often cluster around geopolitical topics, attacking countries, ideologies, or political figures and amplifying existing international tensions.

\Pop culture

In pop culture subreddits, hostility targets celebrities, fandoms, and media franchises, with haters using irony and sarcasm to police taste and status.

\Self improvement & mental health

Even in support oriented spaces, haters undermine vulnerable users by mocking struggles, questioning sincerity, or pushing toxic ‚Äútough love‚Äù narratives.

\Reddit core

Some haters operate in meta communities focused on Reddit itself, attacking other subreddits, moderation decisions, and users who challenge platform norms.

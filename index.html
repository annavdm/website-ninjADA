Introduction

Online conflict is rising, and social media often turns into a stage for anonymous negativity. Using a Reddit dataset (2014–2017), we trace how subreddits link to each other and reveal a hidden network of hostility. Focusing on the 10% of interactions tagged as negative, we dive into the profiles of the users who spread this hostility: their communities, writing styles, targets, and posting habits. By uncovering how these “haters” operate, we aim to shed light on the dynamics that fuel online anger.
